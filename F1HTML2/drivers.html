Drivers of the Turbo-Hybrid Era

Sebastian Vettel

The four-time world champion.
Drove for RB in 2014 then went to Ferrari and ended carreer in Aston Martin.

Lewis Hamilton / Mercedes

The seven-time world champion.
Drove for Mercedes and dominated whole Turbo-Hybrid era.

Nico Rosberg

The one-time world champion.
Drove for mercedes alongside Hamilton. He won in 2016 in equal machinery and then retired.

Daniel Ricciardo

Drove for RB and was said by many that he would beat Hamilton for the title, but RB prioritized Verstappen and he went to Renault.

Max Verstappen

The three-time world champion.
He ended mercedes reign over formula1 and is now one of the best drivers ever.

Kimi Raikkonen

The last champion with Ferrari.
He ended his carreer and went into rallye but came back in 2012 with lotus and then drove again for Ferrari alongside Vettel.

Valtteri Bottas

Teammate of Lewis Hamitlon. Could have challenged Hamilton for the championship but Mercedes didnt let him.

Nico Hulkenberg

Was destined to win championships but in the end didnt even get a podium because how unlucky he was.

Fernando Alosno

The two-time world champion who went back to mclaren, but mclarens performance was really poor till 2020 season.

Jenson Button

The one-time champion with Brawn. Drove for Mclaren alongside Alonso, but the car wasnt good.

Sergio Perez

Drove for ForceIndia and RacingPoint, now he is teammate of Verstappen.

Charles Leclerc

Charles is predestined by many to be the next Schumacher of Ferrari. He also got the longest cotract in history.
